The train from Osaka gets in before the crowds do. I walked from the station toward the river while the shops were still pulling up their shutters, and for twenty minutes it felt like the whole city was mine.

## What I'd do again

- Coffee before anything else, standing up, at a counter.
- Skip the famous temple at noon; go at opening or not at all.
- Let one wrong turn become the day's plan.

## What I'd skip

The souvenir arcades. You already have the photos.
